Please make sure that you have enough time for this experiment. In order to receive the payment of 17 chf it is required that you finish the experiment according to these instructions. You will then receive a coupon with a code that can be exchanged for cash without disclosing your name or signature.

The experiment

This experiment involves two participants. We will call them participant A and participant B from now on. There are 1000 trials that have to be solved. Each of this trial is a simple arithmetic calculation, like “2+2 = 4”.

Participant A will decide how to share the 1000 trials between both participants. Participant B will be asked to accept or reject the proposal of participant A.

In case of rejection the number of calculations will increase and the computer will decide how to share the amount of work between the two of you.

Starting the experiment

After reading these instructions, please click on the START button below in this screen. If the other participant hasn’t done so yet, you will have to wait for him or her to do so.

important: It might take a while for the other participant to read the instructions, so please wait. The screen will refresh automatically. During this period, you can browse the web in another window of the browser or read a book, but please keep an eye on the screen. A sound will be played to notify the experiment has started.

Afterwards, you will be taken to this screen (see picture below), where you will have to

solve 3 trials. This test will let you familiarize with the full work. After this test, you will be taken to the proposal phase.

Proposal phase


If you are participant B, you will have to wait. The page will refresh automatically.

If you are participant A, you will see the screen below.

If you are participant A, you can use the slider in the middle of the screen to select a division of the work. The initial position of the slider is random and has no importance.

On the left panel is listed your proposal. The right panel shows the amount of work both of you will have to if participant B rejects the proposal.

To submit your choice to participant B, please click the button send proposal.

This will take you to the Reply phase.

 

Reply phase

At this point, if you are participant A, you will have to wait. If you are participant B, this is the screen you will see:

 

Please click ACCEPT or REJECT to reply to the proposal of participant A. After this, the work phase will take place.

 

 

Work phase

Both participants will be taken back to the trial screen. If you are participant A, you will be notified of the decision of participant B. After that, both participants can start solving the calculations.

If a wrong value is inserted, you will be asked again to insert the correct value. Please note that you will receive the payment only if you solve all trials you have been assigned.

Thank you, and we hope you will enjoy this experiment!
